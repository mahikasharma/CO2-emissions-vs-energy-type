# CO₂ Emissions vs. Energy Types

**DS 4200 Final Project**  
Mahika Sharma, Kiswa Khan, Neha Maan  

## Overview
This project explores the relationship between energy consumption, energy source, and CO₂ emissions across U.S. states from 1981 to 2023. Our central question:

> **Do states with higher energy consumption have worse CO₂ emissions, or are emissions more strongly influenced by the type of energy source used?**

## Data Source
All data comes from the **U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA)**, which tracks state-level energy consumption and CO₂ emissions across the United States.

## Visualizations
1. **Choropleth Map** — CO₂ emissions intensity by state
2. **Line Plot** — CO₂ emissions trends over time with state dropdown
3. **Scatter Plot** — Renewable energy share vs. CO₂ emissions by region
4. **Boxplot** — CO₂ emissions distribution by energy source and region
5. **Stacked Bar Chart** — Energy mix by region with year slider
